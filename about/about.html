BIO Trinity Martin Trinity is an IT professional and a Transgender Women who recently changed her name from Katie Martin. Her lists of past employers and clients include Skype, Microsoft and Avanade. A full resume is available on request.

Trinity prides herself on being versed in all aspects of Quality software development from coding, through project management, recruitment and delivery on various cloud platforms. Trinity has delivered a number of projects into the market place ranging from start-up venture software to cloud based enterprise platforms. Trinity is well versed in Agility and its numerous component variations ranging from Extreme Lean to Scrum. Trinity was fortunate to work for Thoughtworks and received a good grounding in Agility from people such as Dr Webber and Martin Fowler in her earlier years of software development. Trinity has a love affair with well crafted software and truly believes in Quality as the driving factor of the software production triangle.
